Founder & CEO

Vikas Sharma

Vikas Sharma is the Founder & CEO of Avanti Tour and Travels. With a vision to provide safe, reliable, and premium travel services, he established the company to deliver exceptional travel experiences across India.

Under his leadership, Avanti Tour and Travels focuses on customer satisfaction, safety, punctuality and premium transportation services for corporate travel, family trips, weddings, school tours and outstation journeys.
