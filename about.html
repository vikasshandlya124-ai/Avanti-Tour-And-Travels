Founder & CEO

Vikas Sharma

Vikas Sharma is the Founder & CEO of Avanti Tour and Travels. With a vision to provide safe, reliable, and premium travel services, he established the company to deliver exceptional experiences for corporate travel, family trips, weddings, school tours, and outstation journeys.

His focus on customer satisfaction, transparent pricing, punctual service, and well-maintained vehicles has helped Avanti Tour and Travels build lasting relationships with clients across India.
